Cedar Hill Studios

Mixing and music production

About

Cedar Hill Studios is the mixing home of Michael McCarthy — a multi-instrumentalist, producer, and mixing engineer with a deep musical background and over a decade of experience working across genres.

Before launching Cedar Hill Studios, Michael was an engineer at Range Recording Studios, where he learned professional tracking, editing, and mixing workflows while working alongside experienced engineers and diverse artists.

Michael is also a classical and jazz-trained trumpet player, and that strong musical foundation carries into every mix — bringing instinctive attention to dynamics, phrasing, arrangement, and emotional impact.

As a multi-instrumentalist with hands-on experience in rock, alternative, singer-songwriter, acoustic, jazz, and modern production styles, Michael approaches each project with both a musician's ear, an engineer's precision and a performer's heart.

Cedar Hill Studios exists to help artists bring their recordings to life with clarity, punch, and a modern, release-ready sound.
