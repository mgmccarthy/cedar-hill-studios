About

Cedar Hill Studios is the mixing home of Michael McCarthy — a singer-songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, producer, and mixing engineer with decades of experience across genres. After cutting his teeth as a working engineer at Range Recording Studios, Michael developed a strong foundation in professional tracking, editing, and mixing while collaborating with experienced engineers and diverse artists. A classically and jazz-trained trumpet player, he brings a musician's ear and an engineer's precision to every mix, focusing on dynamics, arrangement, and emotional impact.

With the release of his most recent album (Grand Tortoise's All in Good Time), he's turned his attention to helping other local artists with mixing, production, or just the final push to getting their songs over the finish line.

Send Me Your Tracks!

Curious what your track could sound like with a professional mix? Upload your multi-tracks and I'll create a short teaser mix so you can hear the direction before committing to a full project. There's no pressure — it's simply a way for you to hear how your song could come together.

When sending your track, please include:
  • WAV multitracks (stems)
  • Bit depth (16-bit or 24-bit)
  • Sample rate (44.1 kHz, 48 kHz, etc.)
  • Song BPM / tempo
  • Pro Tools session (if applicable)
Your tracks are kept private and are only used for the purpose of evaluating or mixing your project.
